People have ingredients in their refrigerators but do not know how to
make delicious recipes out of them. Furthermore, there are not a lot of
free apps that allow people to save their own recipes, find recipes,
adjust serving sizes, and see nutritional facts.


When a user clicks on a recipe to make it, the option to choose the
number of serving sizes or to see how many serving sizes they can make
based on how much of each ingredient they have would appear.


Our target audience is adults of all ages. We want this project to be
a mobile application that works with both Android and iOS. Our app will
have to be able to store data locally. If possible, we also want people
to be able to view recipes that other users submit as well, which would
require that we store that information.
